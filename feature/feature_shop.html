SHOP

KiWI enables your community to shop together and place collective orders to save money, time and mind-space​




1. Connect to your favorite retailer​

KiWI lets you connect to your favorite retailer and order​ the essential supplies your community needs.​ Currently we are working hard to provide a large network of different retailers​ and partners to provide you multiple options.

Learn more how to be partner



2. Schedule & announce collective shopping events​

Safe time and costs by collectively ordering all the items you need​
via one single point of sale.​
“I will do the grocery shopping, do you need something?”​
“Hey next time you go shopping, could you bring the chocolate chip cookies?”
​ Simply announce your shopping trip, so others get notified and can join in!​



3. Share & create experiences together

​Are you planning a shared dinner with your friends and group members?​
Great! Let the others know, so they can join!​


Do you have some products left and don’t need them anymore?​
Why don’t you just let others know, so they can take it!
