Sharing is Caring

KiWI enables your community to share experiences at every step


Shop

In a store, it can be challenging to know what to shop if we don‘t remember what we have at home. KiWI will help you with this!

Stock

At home we often struggle to find the right or enough storage space for the products we shopped. Over time, we lose the overview of what we have, how much is left, and where to put things.

Cook

When we cook, we don‘t know which ingredients are available or what we can cook with what we have. Failing to use the products befroe they expire leads to food waste. KIWI can help to prevent that!​
